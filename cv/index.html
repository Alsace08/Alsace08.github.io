Basic Information and Research Direction

Profile

Hello, I'm Yiming Wang.

I'm born in Jiangsu Province, China, in Augest 2001. I'm now a first-year Ph.D. candidate of Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU), supervised by Prof. Rui Wang. Before that, I received a Bachelor's degree from Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Beihang University(BUAA).

Research

I mainly focus on these areas oriented towards Large Language Models (LLMs) and Machine Learning (ML):

1. Complex Reasoning (Primary):

  • Pushing the Upper Limits of LLMs' Known Reasoning Ability [Meta-Reasoning, ACL 2024]
  • Exploring the Boundaries of LLM Reasoning Ability [OOD Detection in MATH, Arxiv 2024]

2. Discrete Generation:

  • Text Generation: Summarization [SumCoT, ACL 2023]; [CPSUM, COLING 2022]
  • Molecular Generation: Retrosynthesis [RetroDiff, Arxiv 2023]

3. Others: LLM Agent, Computational Linguistics

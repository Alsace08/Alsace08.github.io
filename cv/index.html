About Me

Hello, I'm Yiming Wang.

I'm born in Nantong, Jiangsu Province in Augest 2001. I'm now an undergraduate student of Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Beihang University(BUAA), majoring in artificial intelligence.

I'm an incoming Ph.D. student of Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University(SJTU), supervised by Prof. Rui Wang. I'm fortunate to be the third Ph.D student.
